About Chester

His gadgets

Chesters first gadget "Spicy Dice" allows him to change his current super

His second gadget "Candy Beans" gives him a mystery flavored candy which gives him a random buff (damage, cooldown, healing, or speed boost)

His Star powers

Chesters first star power "Single Bell'O'Mania makes it so the first attack out of his entire attacks sequence does 150% damage

Chesters next star power "Sneak Peek" allows him to always know his next super so you will be prepared to use it when you get it
